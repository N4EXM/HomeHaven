Get Involved

Everyone deserves a home where they feel safe, supported, and understood. Whether you're an advocate, landlord, volunteer, or donor, you can help us make accessible, neurodiversity-friendly housing a reality. Join our mission—because change starts with you.

How to Get Involved

For landlords & property Owners

  • List your accessible or sensory-friendly property
  • Learn how small modifications can make your space welcoming
  • Partner with us for tenant matching and support

For Advocates & Volunteers

  • Help us review and vet housing listings
  • Share your expertise (legal, design, or community outreach)
  • Join our advocacy efforts for policy change

For Organisations & Businesses

  • Help us review and vet housing listings
  • Share your expertise (legal, design, or community outreach)
  • Join our advocacy efforts for policy change

About Us

Solace Housing empowers autistic individuals and people with disabilities to find safe, accessible, and supportive housing. Through personalized matching, verified listings, and dedicated advocacy, we’re creating homes where everyone can thrive.
