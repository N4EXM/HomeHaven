Get Involved

Everyone deserves a home where they feel safe, supported, and understood. Whether you're an advocate, landlord, volunteer, or donor, you can help us make accessible, neurodiversity-friendly housing a reality. Join our mission—because change starts with you.

How to Get Involved

For landlords & property Owners

  • List your accessible or sensory-friendly property
  • Learn how small modifications can make your space welcoming
  • Partner with us for tenant matching and support

For Advocates & Volunteers

  • Help us review and vet housing listings
  • Share your expertise (legal, design, or community outreach)
  • Join our advocacy efforts for policy change

Who we work With

At Home Haven, we believe collaboration is key to creating truly inclusive housing solutions. We partner with a diverse network of individuals, organizations, and communities who share our vision of accessible, supportive living spaces for autistic adults and individuals with disabilities.

Property Owners & Landlords

Progressive landlords and developers who prioritize accessibility and are committed to making their properties welcoming for neurodiverse tenants and people with disabilities.

Nonprofits & Advocacy Groups

Disability rights organizations, autism advocacy groups, and housing nonprofits working to expand accessible housing options and influence policy change.

Service Providers & Professionals

Social workers, occupational therapists, and disability support specialists who help tenants thrive in their homes and communities.

Government & Policymakers

Forward-thinking agencies and officials dedicated to improving housing accessibility standards and funding inclusive living initiatives.
