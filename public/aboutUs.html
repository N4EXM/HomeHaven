About Us

At Home Haven, we believe that home is more than just a place—it’s the foundation for independence, safety, and belonging. Founded with the needs of autistic individuals and people with disabilities in mind, we’re redefining housing accessibility by bridging the gap between unique needs and truly supportive living spaces.

Our Story

Born from firsthand experience with the challenges of finding disability-friendly housing, our platform was built by and for the communities we serve. We combine deep understanding with innovative tools to create housing solutions that truly work - where sensory needs, mobility requirements, and social preferences are prioritized from the ground up, not treated as afterthoughts.

What this means for you:

  • Personalised housing solutions that cater to your unique needs.
  • A supportive community that understands your challenges.
  • Access to resources and tools designed for your success.

What Sets Us Apart

Our platform was co-designed with autistic adults, disability advocates, and caregivers—because no one understands these needs better than the people living them every day.
